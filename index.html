Programme Highlights

Artificial Intelligence and Data Science is the current technology which are rapidly changing the world at very high rate. The basic objectives of this Programme is to train students with the next age of Intelligence and analytics generated by machines, influencing the human lives to help improve efficiencies and augment human capabilities, influencing consumer products with significant breakthroughs in healthcare, manufacturing, finance and retail industries.
